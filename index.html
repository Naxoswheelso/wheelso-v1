Car rental in the Cyclades — Naxos, Paros & Mykonos

Wheelso is a local car rental company serving the Greek islands of the Cyclades. Pick up your car at the airport or port, or get free hotel delivery, with transparent pricing, full insurance, unlimited mileage and free cancellation — no deposit and no hidden fees. Whether you need a small city car or a 4x4 for remote beaches, book online in under a minute and pay later if you prefer.
