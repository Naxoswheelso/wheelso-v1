What's not covered — Full Protection

Full Protection covers all accidental damage during normal use. The following events fall outside coverage and may be charged to your card on file.

  • Driving under the influenceAlcohol, drugs, or any substance that impairs driving ability.
  • Off-road or unpaved roadsDamage caused by driving on dirt tracks, fields, beaches, or any road not paved.
  • Undercarriage damageDamage to the underside of the vehicle caused by road hazards, curbs, or improper use.
  • Excessive interior soilingCleaning costs if the vehicle is returned in an unusually dirty condition.
  • Lost or damaged keysReplacement cost of vehicle keys if lost, broken, or locked inside.
  • Wrong fuel typeDamage caused by filling diesel in a petrol vehicle or vice versa.
  • Undeclared additional driverAny damage caused by a driver not listed on the rental agreement.
  • Gross negligence or wilful damageAny intentional or reckless act that results in damage to the vehicle.
Your card on file will only be charged if one of the above events occurs and is verified. No charge is made at pick-up.
