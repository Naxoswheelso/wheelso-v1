Pickup Locations in Naxos

Naxos Island National Airport (JNX)

Our team meets you at the arrivals hall with your keys ready. Average pickup time is 5 minutes. The airport is located 3 km south of Naxos Town and serves seasonal flights from Athens. After hours pickup is available on request.

Naxos Port (Chora)

If you arrive by ferry from Piraeus, Mykonos, Santorini or Paros, we meet you directly at the port — a 2-minute walk from where the ferry docks. The Naxos Port is the busiest entry point to the island, with daily connections to most Cyclades.

Hotel & Villa Delivery

Free delivery to any hotel, villa or apartment within Naxos Town, Agios Prokopios, Agia Anna, Plaka, Mikri Vigla and other popular beaches. For remote locations (Apollonas, Apeiranthos, Komiaki) a small delivery fee may apply — contact us for a quote.

Driving in Naxos: What You Should Know

Roads & Conditions

The main coastal road from Naxos Town south to Plaka and Mikri Vigla is fully paved and easy to drive. Mountain roads to inland villages (Filoti, Apeiranthos, Halki) are narrow but paved. Some access roads to remote beaches in the south are unpaved — a small SUV is recommended for these.

Parking

Free parking is available in most villages and beaches. In Naxos Town, paid parking is available near the port and Kastro area. Avoid parking in the old town narrow streets — use the main parking lot near the Mitropoleos Square.

Fuel Stations

Several fuel stations operate in Naxos Town and along the main coastal road. Petrol is widely available, but plan ahead if you drive to remote villages — fuel stations are limited in the mountain areas.

Distances from Naxos Town

Agios Prokopios beach: 6 km · Plaka: 9 km · Mikri Vigla: 16 km · Apollonas (north): 35 km · Filoti village: 18 km · Apeiranthos (mountain): 32 km.

Rental Requirements in Naxos

  • Minimum age: 21 years for standard cars, 25 years for SUV and 4x4 vehicles.
  • Driving licence: valid licence held for at least 1 year. EU and international licences accepted. Non-Latin licences (e.g. Cyrillic, Chinese) require an International Driving Permit.
  • ID: passport or national ID.
  • Payment: a credit or debit card in the main driver's name (Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, Google Pay).
  • Deposit: a security deposit is held on your card during the rental period. The amount depends on the car category.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I pick up my rental car in Naxos?

You can pick up at Naxos Island National Airport (JNX), Naxos Port, or get free delivery to your hotel anywhere on the island.

What is the minimum age to rent a car in Naxos?

21 years for standard cars and 25 years for SUV and 4x4 vehicles. You must have held your licence for at least 1 year.

Do I need a 4x4 in Naxos?

A 4x4 is not required for main roads. It is recommended only if you plan to visit remote beaches in southern Naxos with unpaved access roads.

Is fuel included in the rental price?

Fuel is not included. We operate a full-to-full policy: you receive the car with a full tank and return it full.

Can I take the rental car to other islands?

Yes, but you must inform us in advance. We also operate stations in Paros and Mykonos for one-way rentals.

What happens in case of a breakdown?

All Wheelso rentals include 24/7 roadside assistance. Call the number printed on your rental agreement and a partner will reach you anywhere on the island.
