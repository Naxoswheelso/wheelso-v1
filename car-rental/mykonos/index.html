Pickup Locations in Mykonos

Mykonos Airport (JMK)

We meet you at the arrivals area with your keys ready. The airport is located 4 km from Mykonos Town (Chora) and serves year-round flights from Athens plus seasonal European routes. Average pickup time is 5 minutes. After-hours pickup is available on request.

Tourlos Port

If you arrive by ferry from Piraeus, Rafina, Naxos, Paros or Santorini, we meet you at Tourlos port — 3 km from Chora and a 5-minute drive. Mykonos is one of the busiest ferry destinations in the Aegean, with daily connections to most Cyclades islands and the mainland.

Hotel & Villa Delivery

Free delivery to any hotel or villa anywhere on Mykonos — whether you're staying in Chora, Psarou, Elia, Ano Mera, or a remote villa in the hills. We coordinate with your accommodation so the car is waiting when you arrive.

Driving in Mykonos: Local Tips

Roads & Beaches

The main roads connecting the airport, Chora, and major beaches are fully paved. Some access roads to luxury villa areas and remote beaches like Agios Sostis and parts of Panormos are unpaved — a small SUV is recommended if you're heading to those areas.

Chora Parking

Mykonos Town has almost zero in-town parking. Leave your car in the designated lots near Fabrica Square or near the windmills, then walk into town. Driving inside the narrow alleys of Chora is not recommended and in many areas is prohibited.

Traffic & Wind

August evenings see heavy traffic around Chora and the Psarou road. The meltemi wind can be strong in July and August — good to know if you're renting a cabrio. Check conditions before driving on exposed coastal roads.

Distances from Chora

Airport: 4 km · Tourlos Port: 3 km · Psarou beach: 5 km · Paradise beach: 6 km · Super Paradise: 7 km · Elia: 10 km · Ano Mera village: 8 km · Agios Sostis: 9 km.

Night Driving

Drive carefully around bars and clubs, especially on weekends. The Psarou–Paradise area can be busy late at night. Watch for pedestrians and scooters. Some rural roads are unlit.

Fuel Stations

Fuel stations are limited and mostly located between Chora and Ano Mera. If you're planning a full day of beach hopping, fill up before you leave town in the morning.

Going to Psarou or Paradise Beach?

Psarou and Paradise are Mykonos's most famous beach club destinations, and both are easily reached by car in under 10 minutes from Chora. Psarou has limited parking — arrive early or use the designated parking area above the beach. Paradise beach has a larger car park near the entrance. If you're heading to Super Paradise, take the signed turn-off from the main Paradise road — the last section is a narrow downhill.

Rental Requirements in Mykonos

  • Minimum age: 21 years for standard cars, 25 years for SUV and 4x4 vehicles.
  • Driving licence: valid licence held for at least 1 year. EU and international licences accepted. Non-Latin licences (Cyrillic, Chinese, Arabic, etc.) require an International Driving Permit.
  • ID: passport or national ID.
  • Payment: credit or debit card in the main driver's name (Visa, Mastercard, Apple Pay, Google Pay).
  • Deposit: a security deposit is held on your card during the rental. The amount depends on the car category.

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can I pick up my rental car in Mykonos?

We offer pickup at Mykonos Airport (JMK), Tourlos Port, or free delivery to your hotel or villa anywhere on the island.

Do I need a car in Mykonos?

Yes, especially if you're staying outside Chora. Most beaches and villas are 10–20 minutes away, with limited public transport in the evenings.

Is parking difficult in Mykonos Town (Chora)?

Yes. Chora has almost no parking. Use the lots near Fabrica Square or the windmills and walk in. Driving inside the old town alleys is not recommended.

Should I rent a SUV in Mykonos?

If you're staying at a villa or visiting Agios Sostis or Panormos with unpaved access roads, yes. For Psarou, Paradise, and Super Paradise, a standard car is fine.

What is the minimum age to rent a car in Mykonos?

21 years for standard cars, 25 years for SUV and 4x4. Your licence must be held for at least 1 year.

Is fuel included in the rental price?

No. We operate a full-to-full policy: you receive the car with a full tank and return it full.
